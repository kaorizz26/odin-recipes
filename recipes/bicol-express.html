Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish hailing from the Bicol region. It is made with pork cooked in coconut milk, shrimp paste, and chili peppers, resulting in a rich and fiery flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 500 grams pork belly, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 4-6 red chili peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add the pork strips and cook until they turn light brown and slightly crispy.
  4. Stir in the shrimp paste and mix well with the pork.
  5. Add the sliced chili peppers and cook for a few minutes to release their flavors.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk and bring it to a simmer. Let it cook for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  7. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste.
  8. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice.
