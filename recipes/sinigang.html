Pork Sinigang

Pork Sinigang is a popular Filipino soup dish known for its savory and sour flavor. It is made with tender pork, vegetables, and tamarind broth, resulting in a comforting and delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork ribs or belly
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 10 cups water
  • 200g tamarind, dissolved in 1 cup water (or 2 tbsp tamarind powder)
  • 200g radish, sliced
  • 200g string beans, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 2 long green chili peppers
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, bring the water to a boil. Add the pork chunks and simmer for about 1 hour or until the meat is tender.
  2. Add the onions and tomatoes to the pot and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the tamarind broth (or tamarind powder dissolved in water) and fish sauce. Stir well and let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the radish and cook for 5 minutes, then add the string beans and eggplant. Simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper according to your taste preference.
  6. Add the long green chili peppers and cook for another 2 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice.
